Controls

Use commands look_around, go, pick_up and put_down to interact with the world. You can use help command to display relevant information.

Goal

Your goal is to destory three rooms with corresponding item. For example you can destroy paper room with scissors by putting scissors down on the floor in the paper room. But be careful. If you put down item which looses agains the room you die a horrible death!
